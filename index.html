Activities to Pass the Time

by Ryan Kim

About

Now that everyone is stuck at home due to quarantine, I suddenly found myself with a lot more time on my hands. The issue was that I did not know what to do with this extra time.

I have thought of a few activities that I would enjoy during these times of quarantine. I hope you can enjoy these as well!

Activities

Want to go camping with friends? Click below! Want to hold a circus or talent show? Click below! Want to play video games on a console or computer? Click below!

Though there are many more activities I can think of such as going to the beach, they all break the rule of social distancing, so it would be pointless to consider doing them now. However, I hope that these few corona-pro activities are enough for you to spend your time away on!

Camping

Go along with a few of your friends on a quick and isolated camping trip! There would be no risk as long as you plan ahead and make sure that the camping spot is isolated from other people.

Host Your Own Show

You also need a few friends for this one, but you could enjoy your time with a fun show with possibly some circus acts, talents, or games for everyone to play together! It doesn't have to be in a circus tent though that would add even more to the fun.

Play Videogames

This is my personal favorite, and it is also something that you can enjoy alone! There are many free games online that you can download or play on a web browser, and playing these games quickly eats away at the hours. Some fun games that I enjoy myself are League of Legends, Minecraft, and Warzone.
